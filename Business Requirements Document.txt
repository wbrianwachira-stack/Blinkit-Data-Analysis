1. Project Overview
The objective of this project is to conduct a comprehensive analysis of the sales dataset of Blinkit
 to unearth the company's sales performance, customer satisfaction, and inventory distribution. 
The the use of a number of visualizations and KPI's, the project will be able to highlight insights and optimization opportunities for the company.

2. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
To measure success, the following primary metrics were identified:
Total Sales: Overall revenue generated from all items sold.
Average Sales: The mean revenue per transaction.
Number of Items: Total count of unique items sold in the inventory.
Average Rating: The mean customer rating for items sold.

3. Visual Requirements & Business Logic
The dashboard must address the following business questions:
Fat Content Analysis: How does fat content (Low Fat vs. Regular) affect total sales and customer ratings? (Donut Chart)
Item Type Performance: Which product categories drive the most revenue? (Bar Chart)
Outlet Performance: How do sales vary across different outlet sizes, locations, and establishment years? (Stacked Column/Line/Pie Charts)
Geographic Distribution: What is the sales spread across different tiers of cities? (Funnel Map)
Granular Metrics: A comprehensive breakdown of all KPIs by Outlet Type. (Matrix Card)
